My story

Hi, my name is Stana, and I help people who want to live an energized healthy life based on plant-based nutrition. Seven years ago, I went through chemotherapy, and that was the point in my life when everything changed. My life transformed massively. The journey of self-discovery started.

My story

This is why I decided to share with you my experience of changing my life and diet and guide you through my four weeks long program of creating new eating habits. During these four weeks, we will meet twice a week for 2 or 3 hours. The program will help with preventing any health problems, detox your body and increase your energy levels.

What to expect

During the 4 week course, you will be invited to enter a sacred space in which you can access your feelings and thoughts about your health and daily habits in order to create a healthier and more conscientious lifestyle.

Together, we will explore the cause of any negative eating habits that have been created and find ways of overcoming these. I will support you to create a sustainable and healthier awareness towards your diet.

The programme

Each personalised programme usually consists of :


  • Dietary Information
  • Tools for personal growth
  • Recipes and sample menus
  • A non-judgemental space to allow feelings and emotions to surface
  • Detoxification and fasting
  • Follow-up support and feedback
